## Priority Actions

### High Impact (Do First)

1. **Add year to titles** - Adding "2025" or "[2025 Research]" establishes freshness and captures year-specific searches. Priority: Pubs 2, 4, 7.

2. **Optimize meta descriptions** - Current summaries are good but can be more keyword-rich and action-oriented. All 6 posts need updated meta descriptions per recommendations above.

3. **Implement cross-linking strategy** - Add 2-3 internal links to each post pointing to related theme posts. Use keyword-rich anchor text.

4. **Add FAQ sections** - Search engines increasingly feature FAQ results. Add 3-5 questions per post that capture "how to" and "why" searches.

### Medium Impact (Do Second)

5. **Restructure H2 headings** - Front-load keywords in heading tags. Example: "The Discovery" becomes "Why the 40% Context Rule Matters."

6. **Add comparison tables** - Pub 4 (framework comparison), Pub 7 (coding agents vs general AI). These capture comparison search intent and often appear in featured snippets.

7. **Expand action sections** - "What You Can Do" sections should include specific, numbered steps. Google favors numbered lists for featured snippets.

### Lower Impact (Do When Possible)

8. **Add schema markup** - Implement Article, FAQ, and HowTo schema where appropriate.

9. **Create downloadable assets** - Checklists, templates mentioned in posts drive backlinks and social shares.

10. **Optimize image alt text** - All images should have descriptive alt text including primary keywords.
