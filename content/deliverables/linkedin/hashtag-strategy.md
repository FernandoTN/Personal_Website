## Best Practices

### Hashtag Count

- **Optimal:** 5 hashtags per post (LinkedIn sweet spot)
- **Minimum:** 3 hashtags (for shorter posts)
- **Maximum:** 7 hashtags (avoid appearing spammy)

### Hashtag Placement

1. Place hashtags at the end of the post after "---" separator
2. Group all hashtags on a single line for clean formatting
3. Do not use hashtags within the body text (disrupts readability)

### Mix Strategy

- **3 broad hashtags:** High reach, competitive (#AIAgents, #EnterpriseAI, #LLMs)
- **2 niche hashtags:** Lower reach, higher engagement (#ProductionAI, #TechLeadership)
- **Optional 1-2 topic-specific:** Rotate based on post content

### Hashtags to Avoid

| Hashtag          | Reason                          |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------- |
| #AI              | Too broad, low engagement rate  |
| #MachineLearning | Generic, not specific to agents |
| #Tech            | Oversaturated, low value        |
| #Innovation      | Overused, perceived as buzzword |
| #Future          | Vague, lacks specificity        |
| #Trending        | Perceived as engagement bait    |
