## Contingency Plans

### Low Engagement (< 1% engagement rate)

**Actions:**

1. Boost visibility by engaging in relevant LinkedIn groups
2. Comment on trending AI/enterprise posts with link to series
3. Ask existing network for reshares
4. Consider adjusting posting time for next post
5. Review hashtag strategy

### Negative Feedback

**Actions:**

1. Respond professionally and promptly
2. Thank for perspective, offer to discuss further
3. Do NOT delete negative comments unless abusive
4. Use feedback to improve future posts
5. Document concerns for methodology reflection

### Technical Issues (Links broken, blog down)

**Actions:**

1. Have backup links ready (Google Doc version)
2. Monitor blog uptime day-of
3. Test links from mobile and desktop
4. Have Fernando's contact ready for blog issues

### Low Blog Click-through

**Actions:**

1. A/B test different link placements in future posts
2. Add "Full breakdown with stats and quotes" language
3. Consider adding preview of blog content in comments
4. Test whether images increase CTR
