# LinkedIn Engagement Response Templates

## Positive Feedback Responses

### Thank You (General)

"Thanks for reading! Glad this resonated. [Personalized comment about their point]"

### Request for More Info

"Great question! I cover this more in [related post link]. The short answer is..."

### Agreement

"Exactly right. In our research, we found [relevant stat] which supports your point."

## Question Responses

### Technical Question

"Good question. Based on our interviews with [source], the approach is..."

### Clarification Request

"Let me clarify: [restate key point]. The key insight is..."

### Disagreement Response

"Interesting perspective. Our research showed [evidence], but I'd love to hear more about your experience with..."

## Engagement Prompts

### Invite Discussion

"What's been your experience with [topic]?"

### Share Request

"If this was helpful, sharing helps others facing the same challenges."

## Response Timing

- Aim for 24h response window
- Prioritize questions over praise
- Always be professional
